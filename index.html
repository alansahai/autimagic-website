FAQs

Common Questions

How is NeuroBeats different from traditional autism therapy?

NeuroBeats combines the benefits of sensory play therapy with AI-powered personalization and real-time feedback. Our system is 85-90% more affordable, can be used at home with remote therapist guidance, and provides objective data to track progress that traditional methods can't match.

What age range is NeuroBeats designed for?

Our system is optimized for children ages 3-12 on the autism spectrum. The adaptive difficulty levels make it suitable for a wide range of developmental stages within this age group.

Do I need any special training to use NeuroBeats with my child?

No special training is required. The system includes guided tutorials and the mobile app provides step-by-step instructions for each therapy session. We also offer free onboarding support for all families.

How does the pricing work?

We offer flexible pricing options: a one-time purchase of the smart bongos device (₹15,000) with optional subscription for premium app features (₹500/month), or a complete rental package (₹1,500/month) that includes the device and all features. Therapy centers and schools receive volume discounts.

Is NeuroBeats clinically validated?

Yes. Our pilot studies with 200+ children showed statistically significant improvements in motor skills, attention span, and emotional regulation. We're currently conducting larger clinical trials and have published our initial findings in peer-reviewed journals.
